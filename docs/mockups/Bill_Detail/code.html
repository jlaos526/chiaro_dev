Plain-Language Summary Read Full Text

This bill changes how Ohio funds K-12 public schools. It directs an additional $200 million per year to school districts where local property tax revenue falls below the state median.

Roughly 274 of Ohio's 609 districts will receive additional funding. The bill is funded by reallocating existing education appropriations and a new 0.5% surtax on commercial property over $5M valuation. A 5-year sunset clause requires legislative renewal in 2029.
